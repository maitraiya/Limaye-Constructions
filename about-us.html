Who We Are

ABOUT PRASHANT LIMAYE CONSTRUCTIONS


Prashant Limaye Construction, is an experienced general contractor dedicated to providing our clients superior service and a quality product at a fair price. Since 1987, our firm’s success has covered a wide range of project types and complexity including industrial, residential and institutional projects. We offer construction management, general contracting, design/build and development services to fit all needs. Our team welcomes the opportunity to work with you and build a strong relationship for the future. Hollenbach Construction completes work on additions, renovations, and restorations to existing buildings as well as new multi-facilities.

The office and field staff of Prashant Limaye Construction, are educated, trained and skilled individuals who take pride in their work and are committed to providing our clients with what they deserve,
“Quality Product At A Fair Price.”

Dedication and longevity are key ingredients of our staff. Many individuals have been with our firm for more than a decade offering extensive experience to our clients. Prashant Limaye Construction, has earned a reputation for continuous quality and excellence.

COMPANY HISTORY


What started in 1987 as a modest construction division has thrived into what is today a full-service construction company offering construction management, general contracting and development services for a wide range of project types. Our company prides itself in the belief of delivering a quality product at a fair price which was instilled in us from the very beginning and has allowed us to continue our reputation of leadership, dependability and quality service.
